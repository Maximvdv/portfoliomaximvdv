Who am i?

My name is Maxim Van der Veken

I am a 25-year old web and graphic designer who studies Graphic and Digital Media at the Artesis Plantijn Highschool in Antwerp.

In my free time I like to work on private projects, such as photography, webdesigning, visual effects and graphic design.

What do i Do?

At young age I already enjoyed working with computers.It was clear that I was going to search for a profession that involved computers.
I made my first basic website when I was 10 years old together with a friend, and that passion has grown a lot since that day.

Today, I study Crossmedia-design. This education has taught me a lot about the web and even more about graphic design. I have used my knowledge from studies and my own interest in my recent projects. This covers all workarea's I am interested in. Front-end developer & designer, graphic designer and photographer.
